Those ads you’ve seen for "one-carat total weight" diamonds or "half carat engagement rings" are deceptive. The weights are rarely exact, and often are exaggerated. Total weight doesn’t mean the weight of the single center stone but the sum of ALL the stones--so it’s hard to know what you’ve really bought. This practice is not only deceptive, it may also be illegal.

At Fortunoff, we weigh and mark every engagement ring with the exact carat weight (to a one-hundredth of a carat). You’ll know the exact weight of the center stone and the exact weights of the side stones.
